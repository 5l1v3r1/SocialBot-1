Add an Twitter App

To add an app to SocialBot you need to create one via https://apps.twitter.com.

You need a twitter account to do that.

You need to do the following things:

  1. Head to https://apps.twitter.com
  2. Press the button 'Create New App'
  3. Fill out all the fields and press 'Create your Twitter application'
  4. You are now in the details view of your app.
  5. Now you need to decide which permissions your app needs. Do that by changing the Access level in the application settings.
  6. Now we need your Consumer Key and your Consumer Secret. You find them under 'Keys and Access Tokens'.
  7. Press 'I've done that' and fill out the form!

Add a Bot to SocialBot

To create a bot you need to add an user to your app.

To add a user to your twitter app be sure to be logged in as the user you want to add.

By clicking the 'Next' Button you will be taken to twitter to authenticate your user for the app.

You then need to press 'Authorize' and put displayed PIN into the field.

As soon as the process is successfully finished the bot will be added.

Add a User to your Twitter App

To add the user to your app you need to type the pin you got from twitter into the form.

Please also provide a name for your bot.
